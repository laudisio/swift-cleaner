Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Swift Cleaner ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles user information.

1. Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

2. Use of Permissions

The Extension requires certain Chrome permissions solely to provide its functionality:

  • browsingData: Used to clear cookies and cache upon user request.
  • tabs: Used to detect the active tab and perform a hard reload (bypass cache).
  • storage: Used locally to save user preferences (such as Dev Mode settings).
  • host_permissions (<all_urls>): Required to allow clearing cookies for specific sites.

These permissions are used strictly for the extension’s core features and never for tracking or data collection.

3. Data Storage

All operations are performed locally within your browser. The Extension does not send any information to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external APIs.

5. Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this Extension.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via GitHub.
